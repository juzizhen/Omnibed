# OmniBed | 拼好床 🛏️

Break free from vanilla Minecraft’s rigid “sleep straight” rules! **OmniBed** lets players lie down in any direction across multiple connected beds, bringing you and your friends the most authentic “big bed rolling” experience in multiplayer.

**👉[中文 README_zh](https://github.com/juzizhen/Omnibed/blob/main/README_zh.md)👈**

## ✨ Core Features

* **Omnidirectional sleeping**: Supports sleeping across two adjacent beds. Whether sideways or diagonally, as long as your facing direction (front, back, left, right) has two continuous empty bed spaces, your body will automatically align perfectly with the bed layout.  
* **Smart bed allocation**: No more fighting over bedheads in multiplayer! If the bed you click is already occupied, the system will intelligently “slide” you into a nearby empty bed space.  
* **Dual respawn protection**: Your respawn point is bound to **two** bed blocks at once. Even if half of the bed is destroyed, as long as the other half remains, your respawn point stays valid.  
* **Perfect multiplayer sync**: Built on native entity DataTracker synchronization, other players will see your “sprawled out” sleeping pose exactly as it is — no clipping issues.  
* **Minimalist and pure**: Ultra-lightweight design,seamlessly fitting into the vanilla survival experience.  

## 🎮 How to Use

Exactly the same as vanilla — zero learning curve:  
1. Place two or more beds side by side (any colors, any arrangement).  
2. Stand next to the bed and **right-click** in the direction you want to lie down.  
3. Enjoy your giant bed!  

*(Note: If any bed block under you is destroyed while sleeping, you’ll be safely awakened.)*

## 📦 Requirements

* **Runtime**: Fabric  
* **Game Version**: 1.20.1  
* **Dependency**: Fabric API  
* **Installation**: Must be installed on both **client** and **server** to ensure animation sync and physics validation.
